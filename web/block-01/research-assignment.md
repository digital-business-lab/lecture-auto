# Industry Research Assignment

**Block 1 · Group assignment · Presentation during Block 2**

---

## Overview

AGVs are used across many industry sectors — but the specific requirements, challenges, and solutions differ significantly between an automotive plant and a hospital, or between a pharma warehouse and an e-commerce fulfilment centre.

Your task is to investigate AGV applications in **one specific industry** and present your findings to the group.

---

## Your Task

### Step 1 — Research (approx. 1–2 hours)

Investigate how AGVs or AMRs are used in your assigned industry. Focus on:

- **Use cases:** What transport tasks do AGVs perform in this sector? What do they move, where, and why?
- **Real examples:** Find at least **two concrete examples** — either a specific company deployment or a documented system installation. Mention the company, system/vendor, and what was achieved.
- **Technical requirements:** What demands does this industry place on AGVs? (e.g. cleanroom conditions, cold chain, explosion protection, payload requirements, 24/7 operation)
- **Challenges:** What are the specific difficulties of deploying AGVs in this sector? (e.g. irregular floor surfaces, human co-workers, regulatory requirements)

### Step 2 — Prepare a Short Presentation

Prepare a presentation of **approx. 10 minutes** (+ 5 min questions).

**Structure:**

1. Industry overview (1–2 slides): size, logistics intensity, key characteristics
2. AGV use cases in this industry (2–3 slides): what tasks, what systems, with images
3. Two concrete examples (1–2 slides): company/system, what was deployed, outcomes
4. Technical requirements and challenges (1 slide)
5. Your conclusion (1 slide): Is AGV deployment in this sector mature, growing, or niche? Why?

**Format:** Slides (PowerPoint or similar). Upload to Moodle before your presentation session.

### Step 3 — Present

Presentations are scheduled during Block 2 (Sessions 3–7). Your slot will be confirmed by the lecturer.
