# Assessment — Business Case

**Weight: 30 % · Submission: semester break**

!!! info "Template coming soon"
    The business case template and grading rubric are being prepared and will be published here.

## Overview

You will develop a structured business case for the introduction of an AGV system in an intralogistics scenario.

## Components

| Component | Description |
|---|---|
| **Market research** | Overview of available AGV systems, vendors, and market trends |
| **Business case calculation** | ROI, payback period, TCO, cost-benefit analysis |
| **Requirements specification** (Lastenheft) | Structured procurement requirements |
| **Utility value analysis** (Nutzwertanalyse) | Weighted comparison of at least two AGV alternatives |
